Character Summary: Lugri Steele

  • Name: Lugri Steele
  • Race: Half-Tiefling (human-like appearance with subtle infernal traits such as glowing yellow eyes and pinkish skin)
  • Class: Cleric (War Domain)
  • Alignment: Chaotic Neutral
  • Deity: A shadowy and manipulative goddess inspired by Kina (DM’s adaptation of her).

Backstory

Lugri Steele is a Half-Tiefling born into a humble family plagued by relentless oppression. His infernal bloodline, inherited from a long-forgotten ancestor, made him an outcast in his human community, further exacerbating his family’s hardships. When local thugs began terrorizing them, Lugri turned to a secretive cult that worshipped a dark and ancient goddess. The cult promised him power in exchange for servitude, allowing him to annihilate his enemies.

However, Lugri quickly realized the goddess sought to dominate him entirely. Refusing to yield his will, Lugri forged a parasitic connection with her instead—siphoning divine energy to fuel his abilities while constantly resisting her influence. This battle of wills has shaped him into a cunning, pragmatic, and fiercely independent individual. Though often morally ambiguous, Lugri remains loyal to those he deems deserving of his trust and protection.

Now, he roams the world as a dark cleric, wielding the goddess’s power against her own designs. His tarnished holy symbol and battle-worn armor stand as a testament to his defiance and his willingness to face any foe, mortal or divine.

Details

Appearance:

  • Lugri is a Half-Tiefling with a mostly human-like appearance, including pinkish skin, glowing yellow eyes, and faint infernal undertones. His horns are nearly imperceptible, blending into his dark, slightly wild hair.
  • He wears dark leather robes adorned with sigils and runes, along with a tarnished holy symbol that he subtly altered to signify his rejection of full servitude.
  • He wields a spiked mace and a shield etched with glowing runes, and his confident, battle-ready stance reflects his brutal pragmatism.

Personality:

  • Lugri has a bard-like charisma with a morbid edge, often using dark humor and blunt honesty in his interactions.
  • He is quick to act, prioritizing immediate solutions over long-term plans, and can make morally gray decisions without hesitation.
